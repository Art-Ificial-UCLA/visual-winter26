DATA SOURCES
All external sources used in this toolkit
🎨 Color Palettes
Adobe Color
color.adobe.com

Trending and curated cinematic palettes. Used via manual curation and their public Explore API. Palettes are community-shared under Adobe's Terms.

🎨 Color Palettes
Coolors.co
coolors.co

Color scheme generator. Palettes exported manually as hex arrays and tagged by mood/genre. Free to use under Coolors' public sharing model.

🎨 Color Palettes
Lospec
lospec.com/palette-list

Community palette database specializing in retro, indie, and pixel-art-influenced color sets. Used for lo-fi, indie film, and stylized genre palettes.

🎵 Music
Free Music Archive
freemusicarchive.org

Community-curated Creative Commons music. Tracks used are CC BY or CC0 licensed. Attribution shown on each detail page as required.

🎵 Music
ccMixter
ccmixter.org

Creative Commons remix community with thousands of tracks. Music is CC BY or CC BY-NC licensed — ideal for indie, folk, and ambient cinematic layering.

🎵 Music
Pixabay Music
pixabay.com/music

Royalty-free music, no attribution required. Licensed under Pixabay's Content License — free for commercial and non-commercial use.

🔊 Sound Effects
Freesound.org
freesound.org

Massive Creative Commons SFX library. Accessed via Freesound API v2. Licenses vary (CC0 / CC BY / CC BY-NC) — shown per track.

🔊 Sound Effects
BBC Sound Effects
sound-effects.bbcrewind.co.uk

16,000+ archival SFX from the BBC archives. Available under the RemArc license for personal, educational, and research use.

🖼 Visual References
FilmGrab
film-grab.com

Curated movie stills used as visual mood references only — not downloaded or hosted. Each reference links back to the original FilmGrab page.

🖼 Visual References
Unsplash
unsplash.com

High-quality CC0 photography used for vibe card backgrounds and visual references. Free for commercial use, no attribution required.
